    • About Us

    More than food – it’s a flavorful journey of tradition and togetherness.

    The Thali Yard was founded in 2021 with a single mission — to revive the charm of traditional Indian meals and serve them in a way that feels like home. We believe food is not just nourishment, but an experience that connects people, evokes emotions, and celebrates heritage.

    Inspired by the diverse flavors of India, our thalis bring together regional delicacies on one plate—balanced, wholesome, and made with love. From Maharashtrian spice to South Indian comfort, every thali reflects authenticity and attention to detail.

    Our restaurant is not just a place to eat — it's a space where families gather, memories are made, and food is served with a smile. Whether you dine in, take out, or book us for an event, we promise quality, taste, and warmth in every bite.
